What's new:

• Fix: deleting an arrow from a finished session now correctly drops
  it from the total score and arrow count. Before, the Total row kept
  including the deleted arrow in the denominator (e.g. 259/310 with
  30 arrows left).
• Fix: 10-ring sessions now render the correct target face in the
  heatmap. Previously the historical detail always drew the 6-ring
  face regardless of the session's actual target, so dots landed on
  the wrong rings visually.
• Fix: Quick Edit Score now keeps the persisted compass direction
  in sync with the snapped dot position. Re-scoring across octants
  used to leave the zone metadata stale, which would surface in
  future analytics that bucket by compass direction.
